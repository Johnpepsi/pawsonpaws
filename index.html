Welcome to PawsonPaws.com

Goosecreek Bernese Mountain Dogs are located in the beautiful town of Rockglen, SK. Our dogs are family members first and foremost. Our puppies are raised in the house with myself, my husband, and our three young children.

As a new breeder it has always been dream of mine that I could improve the health and longevity of this lovely breed by doing what necessary health testing that is required. We breed for health, temperament and longevity of our puppies. I am proud member of the CKC and BMDCC.

Please contact & follow us for more! Facebook.com/Goose-Creek-Bernese 

We are a pure bred Bernese Mountain Dog breeder located in South Central Saskatchewan.
